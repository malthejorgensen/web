CHAOS:\_ is an open source collaboration. We work to lower the cost of disseminating cultural heritage online. Together we have developed technologies that helps us archive, search and present videos, sounds, images and text on the internet.

You can join us.

  • Find us on GitHub
  • Follow @weareallchaos
  • Write us

Who are we?

The Cultural Heritage Project within the Danish Broadcasting Corporation and a range of universities and museums are collaborating within CHAOS:\_.

Who can join?

If you would like to make your own or someone elses cultural heritage available online, CHAOS:\_ could be a project for you. If you're a developer and curious, we'd really like to hear from you.

Where's
the code?

The software developed in the collaboration is available on github.com/CHAOS-Community, where you'll find Software Development Kits for developers, you can see how the project progresses, and you can see examples on how CHAOS:\_ can be used for getting lots of content into your website or your mobile app.
